Variable Names:

These are all for obtained marks. To make them maximum marks just ad '_b' to everything. Example obtained = ass1, then max = ass1_b

DSA:
assignments = ass1, ass2... assn
project= proD, proT, proI, proC, proQ
labs= lab1,lab2...labn
quiz=quiz1, quiz2...quizn
midterm= mid
final= final
participation = part

DM:
same variables with prefix 'DM' eg DMquiz1 DMass1

CALC:
prefix 'CALC' eg CALCass1 CALCquiz1
project= CALpro
peer review= CALpeer

Modernity:
Prefix 'MOD' eg MODquiz1 MODass1

Sustanability:
prefix 'S' eg Sass1 Spart
